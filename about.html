About Shivajna

Samir Puri is a seasoned astrologer with over seven years of hands-on experience in the field of Vedic astrology. Based in Noida, India, he is the founder of Shivajna, a trusted name in the astrological community with a stellar 4.9-star rating on Google. With a passion for decoding the cosmic patterns that influence human life, Samir has evaluated more than 200 horoscopes for clients across the globe. His client base spans countries such as the USA, UK, Canada, Australia and India, offering him a diverse perspective on how planetary energies manifest in different cultural and personal contexts. Samir’s approach is rooted in traditional astrological principles, combined with a modern understanding of life’s complexities. He specializes in analyzing birth charts, identifying planetary strengths and challenges, and offering practical guidance on career, relationships, finances, and personal growth. His predictions are known for their clarity, precision, and actionable advice. What sets Samir apart is his empathetic communication style and his commitment to empowering individuals. He believes astrology is not just about prediction but also about helping people align with their true potential. Whether you’re seeking answers to life’s big questions or looking for deeper self-awareness, Samir Puri offers a thoughtful, grounded and transformative astrological experience. His growing clientele and glowing testimonials reflect his credibility, dedication and spiritual insight.
